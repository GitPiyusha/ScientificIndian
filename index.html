About Scientific Indian

Established in 2002, Scientific Indian has been a trusted manufacturer of high-quality Engineering Educational Equipment for over two decades.

We specialize in precision-engineered laboratory equipment for Mechanical Engineering departments across colleges and universities in India. Our products are widely recognized for their accuracy, affordability, elegant design, and ergonomic excellence.

From Theory of Machines and Thermodynamics to Heat Transfer, I.C. Engines, Fluid Mechanics, Refrigeration & Air-Conditioning, and Production Engineering — we cover the complete spectrum of mechanical engineering laboratories with working models, cut-section models, and advanced test rigs.

Backed by a dedicated servicing division, we ensure every equipment delivers consistent accuracy through regular upgrades and calibration — a promise we’ve kept since day one.

Proudly supplied and installed in hundreds of prestigious institutions across India for the last 22 years.

“We guarantee to fulfill your requirements — with unmatched quality, precision, and trust.”
